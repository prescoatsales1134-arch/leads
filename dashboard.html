Tutorial

If you're new to the Sales Professional Dashboard, this tutorial will show you exactly how to start generating leads using both LinkedIn and Google sources.

Inside the dashboard, you can:

  • Track how many leads you generated today
  • See your total generated leads
  • Check how many leads were exported to your CRM
  • Monitor your remaining lead limit
  • Generate new leads by industry, country, company size, and more
  • Export your leads to CSV
  • Use AI to generate cold emails for specific leads

What this tutorial covers

1. Understanding your dashboard stats

At the top of the page, you can quickly view your lead generation activity, including:

  • Leads generated today
  • Leads generated overall
  • CRM exported leads
  • Remaining lead credits

This helps you keep track of your usage and know how many leads you still have available.

2. Generating LinkedIn leads

To generate leads from LinkedIn:

  • Go to the Generate Leads tab
  • Select your target industry
  • Choose the country
  • Set company size if needed
  • Choose your max results

For LinkedIn searches, the minimum number of results is 100, and you can go up to 30,000 results depending on your needs. Once submitted, lead generation may take around 1 to 2 minutes.

3. Viewing your results

After your leads are generated, they will appear in the Results tab.

From there, you can:

  • Browse through the leads
  • Review company and contact information
  • Move through results using next/previous navigation

4. Generating AI cold emails

One of the most useful features is the ability to create a cold email for a specific lead.

You can simply take a lead’s name or business and ask the AI assistant to generate a cold email tailored to that lead. Then you can copy the email and paste it directly into Gmail or your outreach tool.

5. Exporting leads to CSV

Once you have your results, you can export them as a CSV file.

This makes it easy to:

  • Upload leads into your CRM
  • Organize outreach lists
  • Share lead data with your team
  • Keep an offline record of generated leads

6. Using the History tab

If your current results tab is empty, you can always go to the History section.

The History tab lets you:

  • View previously scraped leads
  • Filter by date
  • Export older lead lists again
  • Separate leads by source, such as LinkedIn or Google

7. Generating Google leads

You can also generate leads using Google.

To do this:

  • Enter a search term or niche, like real estate
  • Add a target location, such as New York, USA
  • Choose the number of results

For Google leads, the range is much more flexible. You can generate as few as 1 result and go up to 1,000 or more depending on your use case. You can also include nearby places to expand your search results. Just like LinkedIn, these leads will appear in your results and history, and you can export them when needed.

Why this feature is useful

The Sales Professional Dashboard is built to make lead generation faster and more practical for sales teams, agencies, and business owners.

With one system, you can:

  • Find targeted leads
  • Search by niche and location
  • Export lists instantly
  • Access lead history anytime
  • Use AI to write outreach emails faster
